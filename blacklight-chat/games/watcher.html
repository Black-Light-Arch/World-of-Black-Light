The One Who's Watching

About the Game

The One Who's Watching is a dark psychological horror game inspired by the eerie atmosphere of Stranger Things.

Explore abandoned environments, uncover hidden secrets, and survive the presence of something that is always watching you from the shadows.

Features

  • Story-Driven Horror
  • Atmospheric Sound Design
  • Exploration & Mystery
